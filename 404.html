You have stumbled on a mystery. What happened to the content of InfoWars?

It is indeed curious. But consider this: Just because what you were looking for isn’t there, it doesn’t mean what you will find isn’t worthwhile. Some things are not lost, even as they cannot be found.

We stumble around blindly as we grope for the truth. Sometimes we grab a handful of wet, squishy truth. Sometimes the wet, squishy handful is something else altogether.

This much is certain: InfoWars is changing. If you keep coming, you may find things that engage your mind and others that, honestly, will enrage you like always.

Enjoy the mystery while it lasts. All will be revealed when the time is right.
